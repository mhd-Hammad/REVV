INTERIOR

COCKPIT PRECISION

The driver-focused cabin of the SF90 Stradale is a testament to Ferrari's relentless pursuit of perfection. Every surface, every stitch, every switch has been conceived with a single purpose — to place the driver in absolute command. The steering wheel, borrowed directly from Ferrari's Formula 1 programme, houses every critical control within thumb's reach. Carbon-fibre trim panels frame a digital instrument cluster that transforms data into art. Alcantara and the finest Poltrona Frau leather envelop the occupants in a cocoon of Italian luxury, while the racing-derived bucket seats hold the body firmly through every apex. This is not merely a car interior — it is a sanctuary engineered for those who demand the extraordinary.
